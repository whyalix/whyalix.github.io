Camera Workshop in Teufelsberg

Join us on Saturday October 8th (between 11AM and 6PM) for an experimental use of your camera in the historical site of Teufelsberg.

About the workshop

We’ll explore in a small group new perspectives with your camera.

Get out from your habits and get new inspiration. We’ll make some exercises to break the rules, and come up with fresh ideas, both with photo and video capture. The idea is to train your perception of your surroundings, and express it with your camera.

This workshop is opened to all levels.

Pricing & equipment

The workshop costs 45€ per participant. Equipment is not included; you will have to bring your own camera.
